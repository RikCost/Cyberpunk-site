Background

One of Night City's most skilled netrunners, Spider Murphy made her name breaking through corporate ICE that others considered impenetrable. Originally from a nomad family, she left the clan to pursue her talents in the urban jungle, quickly establishing herself as a force to be reckoned with in the Net.

Murphy gained notoriety after the DataKrash of 2020, where she was one of the few runners who managed to extract critical data during the collapse. Her ability to navigate black ICE and create custom intrusion programs has made her both a valuable ally and a dangerous enemy.

Currently believed to be working with various anti-corporate groups, providing access to secure systems and extracting sensitive information. Has been linked to several major data breaches at Arasaka and Militech facilities, though concrete evidence remains elusive.

Background

Morgan Blackhand is widely regarded as the most dangerous Solo in Night City, possibly the world. His reputation for tactical brilliance and combat expertise has made him a legend in mercenary circles. Unlike many Solos who rely on cyberware and brute force, Blackhand is known for his strategic mind and ability to outthink opponents.

Former military special forces operative who went private after the collapse of the United States. Has worked for various corporations and private clients, but maintains strict independence and a personal code that has earned him respect even among enemies.

Blackhand's involvement in the Fourth Corporate War, particularly his confrontations with Adam Smasher, have become legendary. Believed to have been severely injured during the Arasaka Tower bombing, but reports of his death have repeatedly proven premature.

Intelligence suggests Blackhand may be operating from the shadows, potentially gathering resources and allies for an unknown operation. Multiple high-profile mercenary contracts have been traced to shell companies potentially linked to his network.

Background

Johnny Silverhand is one of Night City's most iconic figures - a legendary rockerboy, revolutionary, and anti-corporate terrorist. As the frontman of the chrome rock band Samurai, he used his music and influence to spread anti-corporate messages and incite rebellion against corporate control.

Born Robert John Linder, he served in the Central American Conflict where he lost his arm, later replaced with his signature silver prosthetic that earned him his stage name. After returning from war, he channeled his rage and disillusionment into music, quickly rising to fame with Samurai.

Silverhand's hatred for corporations, particularly Arasaka, intensified after the kidnapping and presumed death of his girlfriend Alt Cunningham, a brilliant netrunner who created the Soulkiller program. This led to his first attack on Arasaka Tower, which failed but cemented his status as a corporate enemy.

His final act of rebellion came during the Fourth Corporate War, where he participated in a raid on Arasaka Tower alongside Morgan Blackhand. Intelligence suggests Silverhand was killed during this operation, though his body was never recovered. Recent unconfirmed reports indicate possible engram preservation of his consciousness.
